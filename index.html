Who We Are?

The Industries Leading Full Service Aircraft Appearance Services and Cabin Maintenance Company

AeroCorp MRO is a maintenance service provider for luxury cabin interiors, categorised under the C6 capability, operating under the approval and guidance of GCAA MOA / GCAA CAR 145 / EASA PART 145.
We provide our clients with high-end luxury completion services to create bespoke interiors that reflect the personalities and lifestyles of our clientele..
Our comprehensive range of services includes, Plastic Parts Repair, Wood Veneer, Hydro Dipping Print, Leather Restoration, Customized Carpets, Fabrics Reupholstery
